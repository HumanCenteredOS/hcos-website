# 🌐 HCOS™ Website

## Human-Centered Operating Systems™

The HCOS™ website is the public home of Human-Centered Operating Systems™.

It exists to help people better understand themselves, one another, and the systems they live within while protecting human dignity, reducing unnecessary human burden, and creating the conditions where people and organizations can flourish.

The website brings together the HCOS™ Foundations, Standards, Insights, Research, Learning, Tools, and Platform into one accessible experience.

Whether you are discovering HCOS™ for the first time or exploring ways to apply it within your organization, the website is designed to make complex ideas understandable, practical, and actionable.

---

# ❤️ Our Purpose

The HCOS™ website serves as the central hub for learning, collaboration, and implementation.

Our goals are to:

- Introduce the HCOS™ Framework
- Share educational resources
- Publish Foundations and Standards
- Explore real-world Insights
- Support responsible AI
- Highlight research and innovation
- Connect people with practical tools
- Build a community dedicated to healthier systems
